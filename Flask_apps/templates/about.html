ABOUT

Landmark Recognition is a technique of recognizing and identifying various landmarks.

Usually when people travel to foreign countries or abstract locations, they click tons of photos.

It becomes difficult to recognize the actual location or landmark after a certain amount of time. Large number of photos from different locations can lead to more confusion about the name of the place.

This model can help people to identify the location from random pictures that they come across from various sources like internet, television, etc.
